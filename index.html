About

17+ years in the trenches

I started writing iOS apps before Apple released the SDK, hacking together Objective-C on jailbroken hardware. Since then I've led platform teams at Wayfair, NewsCorp, Cameo, and Hearst, working at every layer from kernel drivers to SwiftUI.


Outside of work, I maintain several high-profile open source projects (29k+ combined GitHub stars), build emulators that run on Apple Silicon without JIT, and write hardware drivers that ship in RetroArch's mainline iOS/macOS builds. Provenance is now on the App Store with 388k+ downloads. I use AI tooling heavily and build custom MCPs, skills, and automation for my teams.
