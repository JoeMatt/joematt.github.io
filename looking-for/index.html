Roles I want to talk about

Where the conversation is interesting

Six shapes of engagement. If your role looks roughly like one of these, the call will be productive — for both of us.

Full-time

Head of AI Platform / Head of AI Infrastructure

Building the engineering layer between AI capability and product. MCP servers, agent orchestration, eval harnesses, developer tooling. Series B+ companies with real product traction and ~50–500 engineers.

Full-time

VP Engineering (Mobile-led product)

Where mobile is the product or a strategic surface. Series B/C/D scale. Have at least one mobile team to lead. AI integration on the roadmap.

Full-time

Director, Mobile Platform

Multi-region mobile org. Cross-app SDK strategy. Performance, observability, release engineering. The NewsCorp role I held in 2019, scaled up.

Consulting

Fractional / advisory CTO or VP Eng

5–15 hours/month retainer. Architecture sounding board, hiring panels, technical due-diligence, escalation contact. Founders with technical CEOs but no senior IC depth in mobile or AI.

Consulting

Mobile AI Launch Readiness Audit

The productized 3-week engagement on /consulting/. iOS team about to ship AI features and wants an honest pre-launch read.

Consulting

Strategy days / single-day engagements

Architecture review, hiring panel, due-diligence read, conference talk prep. $1,500–4,500/day. Best for “we need an outside read in the next two weeks” situations.

Hard constraints

Yes / no, no ambiguity

If your role doesn't match these, save us both the round-trip. If it does, we should talk.

Yes

  • Series B+ companies with real product-market fit
  • Teams of at least 5 mobile engineers OR a meaningful AI infrastructure mandate
  • A clear technical leader I'd report to (CTO / VP / SVP Eng) for FT roles
  • Compensation in market range for the role (typical NYC Director AI/VP Eng band: $300K–600K+ total comp)
  • Equity that's not obviously underwater
  • Timezones overlapping at least 4 hours with US Eastern

No

  • Pre-PMF / pre-seed startups looking for a CTO co-founder
  • Greenfield rewrites with no engineers to lead
  • Fully-React-Native shops with no native mobile path
  • “We need someone to manage the offshore team” without scope or autonomy
  • Crypto / NFT / gambling / surveillance-adjacent products
  • IC-only roles below Staff level
  • Non-disclosed comp bands (“competitive” without a number)
  • Unpaid trial projects masquerading as interview rounds
